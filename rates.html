Rates

The rate starts at $650 per night and up based on availability. There is a $375 cleaning fee and a $895 refundable security deposit required per stay. Snowshoe and cross-country ski rentals as well as a sledding hill are available on property at the neighboring Clair Tappaan Lodge.


Reservations

Reservation requests are accepted by calling 408-297-5202 or emailing the request to ballegretto@miramarhospitality.com. A valid credit card must be provided when making the reservation. Full payment is required when making the reservation.

Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing starts right out the door. Several downhill skiing areas are in the immediate area with weekend shuttle service. Spring, summer, and fall activities include hiking, swimming in mountain lakes, biking on road and off road, and rock climbing. The Pacific Crest Trail is nearby and Lake Tahoe is just 25 miles away.


Winter Recreation


Every winter sport imaginable occurs here on Donner Summit. Downhill skiing, cross-country skiing and snow boarding are minutes away at Sugar Bowl, Soda Springs, Donner Ski Ranch, Royal Gorge and Boreal. Sugar Bowl Ski resort and Soda Springs can be accessed by a free shuttle and we have ski in\ ski out access to Donner Ski Ranch. Squaw Valley, Alpine Meadows and Northstar Ski Resorts are within easy driving distance.


Spring, Summer, and Fall Recreation


Warm days and cool nights make Summer very enjoyable here in the Sierra Nevada Mountains. An array of outdoor adventures can be enjoyed including mountain biking, hiking, rock climbing, boating, water skiing, canoing, river rafting, fishing, swimming and sailing.

If you want adventure, Lake Tahoe is the place Winter, Spring, Summer and Fall. The Cal Lodge offers a central point with a warm fireplace to access all the recreation in the Lake Tahoe area.
